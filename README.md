# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 13, 2026**

Modern Ouija ("the app") is an offline entertainment utility. Your privacy matters to us, and the app is built so that your information stays on your device. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

## Information We Collect

Modern Ouija does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. We operate no servers and use no analytics, tracking, or advertising. We never see your data.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Your settings — including routines, an imported home-screen screenshot, and any audio you record for the Fake Call feature — are stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

## Photos

If you choose to import a home-screen screenshot, the app accesses it through Apple's standard system photo picker. The selected image is processed and stored locally on your device for use within the app. No photos are ever uploaded or shared.

## Microphone

The optional Fake Call feature lets you record an audio clip to play back during a performance. Any recording you make is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted, uploaded, or shared. The microphone is accessed only while you are actively recording.

## Camera & Flashlight

Some effects use the device's flashlight (torch). The app does not capture, record, or store any photographs or video, and does not access the camera for imaging.

## Network & Third Parties

The app is designed to function entirely offline. The only possible network request is loading an optional tutorial video hosted by YouTube inside the performer instructions, which occurs only if a video has been configured and is subject to YouTube's own privacy practices. No personal data is sent in connection with this.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children or from anyone else, as it does not collect personal information at all.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with a revised date above.
